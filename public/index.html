About us

Living Light Ministries is an interdenominational ministry that serves the body of Christ in Evangelism through Radio, Television and Crusade; Teaching, and Revivals in the Philippines and around the world.

The Living Light Christian Fellowship is a family church that believes in Salvation, Healing, The Baptism and the Gifts of the Holy Spirit.

We also believe that Israel is God's chosen people, the real branch; and that we as wild olives have been grafted in their place in the olive tree. One day God is going to restore Israel to its place.

While waiting for that, it is our duty to pray, intercede, help and love God's chosen people-Israel. We pray for the peace of Jerusalem, God's Holy City.
